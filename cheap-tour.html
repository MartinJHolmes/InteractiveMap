Budget Tour of Valencia

Overview

After breakfast (remember to bring something) we will take the No. 95 bus from the City of Science and Arts to the Old Town. There you'll get a glimpse of some of the Muslim walls.

From there we will walk to Torres de Sarrano and then along the Turia River. Please remember to bring strong shoes, it is a long walk. After arriving at the Port of Valencia you'll be exhausted and ready to return to your hostels for the night.
